# Elite Forums Reference

Curated list of elite/niche developer communities per language. Use for `site:` targeting in research agents.

**Not a fixed list** — discover more per topic via `"[topic] 论坛 社区 精华"` or `"[topic] лучший форум"` queries.

## Chinese (ZH)

### General Developer

| Forum | URL | Signal | Notes |
|-------|-----|--------|-------|
| V2EX | v2ex.com | High | 250K+ creative workers, strict moderation, Google login only |
| 掘金 (Juejin) | juejin.cn | High | Developer blogging + Q&A, ByteDance-owned |
| SegmentFault 思否 | segmentfault.com | High | Chinese Stack Overflow equivalent |
| 知乎专栏 (Zhihu) | zhihu.com | Medium-High | Long-form tech analysis, filter by 专栏 |
| 博客园 (cnblogs) | cnblogs.com | Medium | Original blog content, less noise than CSDN |
| W2Solo | w2solo.com | High | Indie developer community |
| 程序员客栈 | proginn.com | Medium | 820K devs, freelance + remote focus |
