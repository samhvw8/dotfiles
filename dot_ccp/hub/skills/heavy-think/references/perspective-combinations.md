# Perspective Combinations

Pre-built perspective sets for common brainstorming scenarios. Use as starting points — customize to the specific problem.

## Product Direction

**"What should we build next?"**

| Perspective | Lens |
|-------------|------|
| User Advocate | What do users actually struggle with vs what we assume they want? |
| Minimalist | What's the smallest thing that would matter most? |
| Competitor | What would our strongest competitor build if they had our assets? |

**Deep variant** — add:
| Futurist | What's becoming possible in 12-18 months that isn't today? |
| Economist | What has the best effort-to-value ratio given current resources? |

## Architecture Decision

**"Which technical approach should we take?"**

| Perspective | Lens |
|-------------|------|
| Operator | What breaks at 10x scale? What's the 3am pager scenario? |
| First Principles | Strip away convention — what does the problem actually require? |
| Minimalist | What's the simplest approach that won't need replacing in 2 years? |

**Deep variant** — add:
| Historian | What have other teams/companies chosen for similar problems? What happened? |
| Futurist | Which approach positions us best for where the ecosystem is heading? |

## Go-to-Market / Positioning

**"How should we enter this market?"**

| Perspective | Lens |
|-------------|------|
| Economist | Where's the money? What pricing/model captures value? |
| Contrarian | What if the obvious market entry is wrong? Where's the underserved angle? |
| User Advocate | What would make someone switch from their current solution to ours? |

**Deep variant** — add:
| Competitor | Who will respond, how, and how fast? |
| Adjacent | What completely different industry solved a similar distribution problem? |

## Naming / Branding

**"What should we call this?"**

| Perspective | Lens |
|-------------|------|
| User Advocate | What name would a user remember and search for? |
| Contrarian | What names break convention in this space? What's everyone else NOT doing? |
| Futurist | Will this name age well? Does it box us in or leave room to grow? |

## Feature Prioritization

**"What should we do first?"**

| Perspective | Lens |
|-------------|------|
| Economist | What has the highest expected value per unit of effort? |
| User Advocate | What's causing the most pain right now? |
| Operator | What's creating the most operational burden or technical risk? |

**Deep variant** — add:
| Minimalist | What can we cut entirely without meaningful loss? |

## Process / Organizational

**"How should we work differently?"**

| Perspective | Lens |
|-------------|------|
| First Principles | What does effective collaboration actually require vs what we ritualize? |
| Contrarian | What sacred cow is actually hurting us? |
| Historian | What worked when we were smaller/faster? What did we lose scaling up? |

## Composing Custom Sets

When none of the above fit, compose using these rules:

1. **At least one challenger** — Contrarian, First Principles, or Minimalist
2. **At least one stakeholder lens** — User Advocate, Operator, or Economist
3. **At least one temporal lens** — Historian or Futurist
4. **Maximum productive tension** — each perspective should disagree with at least one other
5. **No redundancy** — if two perspectives would say roughly the same thing, cut one
